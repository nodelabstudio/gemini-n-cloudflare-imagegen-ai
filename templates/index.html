Cloudfire

What is this?
Cloudfire is a tool that creates pictures from words. You type a description of what you want to see, pick an AI model, and the app generates an image for you. It's like having an artist that draws whatever you describe!

How does it work?
Your description gets sent to an AI service (either Cloudflare or Google Gemini). The AI has been trained on millions of images, so it knows how to turn words into pictures. It takes about 10-30 seconds to create each image.

What are the limits?

  • You can generate up to 5 images per minute
  • Very detailed descriptions of people may get blocked by safety filters
  • The AI can't perfectly control text inside images
  • Results vary between models — try different ones!
  • Images are saved to your gallery so you can download them anytime

Which model should I use?
Start with FLUX.1 Schnell (free, great quality). If you want the best results and don't mind paying, try FLUX.2 Dev. For Google-powered generation, try Gemini 2.5 Flash.
